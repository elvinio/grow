# Teaching & Parenting Knowledge Base

A resource for Singaporean parents and educators navigating one of the world's most demanding — and most caring — education systems. These guides draw on research in developmental psychology, learning science, and the realities of the Singapore context.

## What's Inside

- **[parenting-philosophy.md](./parenting-philosophy.md)** — Parenting styles, kiasu culture, psychological safety at home, building intrinsic motivation, and how to have honest conversations about grades without damaging your relationship.

- **[teaching-primary.md](./teaching-primary.md)** — Routines, parental involvement, making learning engaging, managing the PSLE year, and knowing when to seek help.

- **[teaching-secondary.md](./teaching-secondary.md)** — Shifting from teacher to coach, supporting teens through subject choices, burnout, O-levels, and the tuition question.

- **[teaching-jc-poly.md](./teaching-jc-poly.md)** — Letting go gracefully, mentoring without controlling, and supporting your child through JC, Poly, or ITE with equal pride.

- **[singapore-education-system.md](./singapore-education-system.md)** — The full MOE pathway explained clearly, from Primary 1 to university and beyond, including recent 2024–2025 reforms.
