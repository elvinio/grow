# Subjects

This section covers the core academic subjects in Singapore's school curriculum, with practical guidance for parents, students, and educators at every level — from primary school through O-levels.

Each file goes beyond syllabus summaries to address how children actually learn each subject, where they commonly get stuck, and what you can do about it.

## What's inside

- **[mathematics.md](./mathematics.md)** — The Singapore Math model method, conceptual vs procedural learning, problem-solving heuristics, number sense, PSLE and O-level topics, and a growth mindset approach to "I'm bad at math."

- **[science.md](./science.md)** — Inquiry-based learning, the PSLE science themes, O-level discipline strategies, lab skills, making abstract ideas concrete, and common exam mistakes.

- **[english.md](./english.md)** — The four skills (reading, writing, listening, speaking), comprehension strategies, composition techniques, navigating Singlish vs Standard English, and PSLE and O-level component breakdowns.

- **[chinese.md](./chinese.md)** — Singapore's bilingual context, character learning strategies, oral and reading exposure, composition writing, and how to make Chinese a living language at home.
