# Foundations

The Foundations section covers the science of learning — what actually happens in the brain, why some study habits work and others waste time, and how mindset shapes everything.

These are not opinions. They are findings from decades of cognitive science and educational psychology, translated into practical guidance for students, parents, and educators in Singapore.
